Bio

Mike Herring began dancing at the age of 6 after seeing chimney sweeps softshoe across the rooftops of London in Mary Poppins. Starting with tap, then adding on various other styles, Mike has become a well known performer and instructor in the Virginia area.

Since 2006 he has been teaching tap at the Wilson School of Dance. In 2009 he co-founded SwingCville, a local dance organization dedicated to preserving vernacular jazz dances like Lindy Hop, Charleston, and Balboa. With this group he is a main organizer for the annual swing dance weekend "The Lindustrial Revolution" which attracts dancers from across the East Coast.

His choreography and performances have been seen at many local shows, fundraisers, and events, such as:

  • Lindy Focus New Year's Eve performance 2013
  • The Wilson School of Dance Spring Performance 2006-2014
  • The Paramount Holiday Spotlight
  • The Charlottesville Ballet
  • Literacy-For-All fundraiser at the Jefferson
  • Onstage at Fridays After Five with ACME Swing Mfg. Co
  • The C-VILLE Best Of Party 2011
  • Fluvanna County Relay for Life 2013
  • Walking Free Fundraiser at Christ Church in 2013.
  • Charlottesville's Nursing Home Swing
  • Choreographer for University Dance Club at UVA, 2003-2007

He is also an occasional collaborator with local jazz musicians such as Stephanie Nakasian and Hod O'Brien, ACME Swing Mfg. Co, and the SwingKats.

His teaching credits include:

  • Instructor at "The Process" in Richmond, VA, 2014
  • Tap Jam leader at Lindy Focus, 2009-2013
  • Instructor at Wilson School of Dance, 2006-present
  • Instructor at SwingCville, 2009-present

Additionally, Mike has taught workshops in Richmond, Norfolk, VA Beach, Williamsburg, Lynchburg, and several nearby universities such as UVA, JMU, and VT.

In 2009 Mike won 1st place in the cabaret division at Lindy Focus for his piece "Tappers Inc", and in both 2009 and 2011 he won 4th place for tap numbers at the International Lindy Hop Championships.
