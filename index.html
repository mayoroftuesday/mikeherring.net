Welcome

Mike Herring is a dancer, instructor, and choreographer in Charlottesville, VA. He started dancing in 1991 and began teaching in 2003, specializing in tap and swing dance. Mike currently teaches at the Wilson School of Dance and at Swing Cville. For more details, view his full bio.
